What is eSIM
An eSIM is a digital SIM that eliminates the need for a physical SIM card. With eSIM, you can quickly and easily transfer an
existing cellular plan or get a new cellular plan, all digitally. You can even store multiple eSIMs on the same device and use
two phone numbers at the same time. And it’s more secure - someone can’t remove the physical SIM card if your iPhone is lost or stolen. In just a few simple steps, you are ready to make calls, send messages, and browse the web. Learn more about
eSIM.

* Use of eSIM requires a wireless service plan.
Not all carriers support eSIM. Use of eSIM in iPhone may be disabled when
purchased from some carriers. Check with your carrier for details and restrictions. Learn more about eSIM and carriers.
